DECON

Tool

DECON is a tool that helps to encode and decode your personal information (simple english text) and save it.


API

https://decon-sand.vercel.app/type/text



1) type:

here you have to options 'encode' (for encoding the text) and 'decode' (for decoding the text).


2) text:
